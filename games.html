Information on the types of Game Developers

Please check below on all the genres currently in the video game world below!

Short for independent games. Indie games are video games made by individuals or small teams without the support of a large game publisher. They are known for being creative, experimental, and often made with lower budgets. Indie developers have more freedom to try unique ideas, which leads to original stories, gameplay, and art styles. Popular indie games include Stardew Valley, Hollow Knight, and Undertale.

Triple AAA Triple-A games are high-budget video games produced by large studios with big teams and major publisher support. They often feature advanced graphics, long development times, and large marketing campaigns. AAA games aim for wide appeal and commercial success, with examples like Call of Duty, God of War, and Grand Theft Auto V.
